# Community Code of Conduct

Cockroach Crawler is an engineering community. Participate with respect, technical honesty, and care for other people's time and safety.

Expected behavior includes constructive review, clear evidence, credit for prior work, respect for provider rules, and patience with contributors of different experience levels. Harassment, threats, discrimination, doxxing, deliberate secret exposure, spam, vote manipulation, and repeated bad-faith disruption are not accepted.

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that violate these standards, and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Report conduct concerns privately to `ajnasnb@gmail.com`. Security vulnerabilities belong in [GitHub Security Advisories](https://github.com/AjnasNB/cockroach-crawler/security/advisories/new), not conduct reports.

Enforcement decisions should protect affected participants, minimize unnecessary disclosure, and document repository-level actions when appropriate.
